About the Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an AI-powered grocery price comparison and optimization system designed to help everyday shoppers make smarter purchasing decisions.

The system allows users to search for grocery items and compare prices across multiple stores. It analyzes historical pricing data and provides intelligent recommendations on where and when to buy groceries at the lowest possible cost.

  • Search for specific grocery items across stores
  • Automatically calculate the total cost of a shopping basket
  • AI-based recommendation of the best store to purchase items
  • Historical price analysis to determine the best time to buy
  • Filter results by location, price, and day of the week
